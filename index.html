Market Research Report — Somatropin / Human Growth Hormone

Candor Capital

Authored a comprehensive market research report on Somatropin (recombinant Human Growth Hormone) commissioned by Candor Capital, with specific emphasis on India-specific growth dynamics, competitive landscape, and commercial opportunity sizing.

The report mapped Indian regulatory pathways through CDSCO for biosimilar and branded rHGH approvals, analysed reimbursement structures, and benchmarked key domestic and multinational players operating in the segment.

Identified critical growth drivers for the Indian market including rising diagnosis rates of GHD, expanding paediatric endocrinology infrastructure, increasing insurance penetration, and favourable government biosimilar policy tailwinds.
